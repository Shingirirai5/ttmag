About

TT Mag repairs is Gauteng's trusted destination for convenient, affordable, and top-quality alloy wheel repairs.
We specialize in a range of services, including diamond cut rim restoration, curb scrape repairs, and custom painted mag wheel refinishing. With our dedicated team of specialists, we bring our expertise directly to you, providing onsite repairs at your home or place of business. we take pride in delivering exceptional results.
Our skilled technicians employ the latest techniques and use high-quality materials to ensure your wheels are restored to their former glory. Whether you have scuffed diamond cut rims that need a flawless finish, curb scrapes that require professional attention, or custom painted mags that demand a pristine look, we have the expertise to handle it all.
